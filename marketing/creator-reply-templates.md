# Reply templates — handling creator responses without AI help

## 1. They say YES

> Awesome! Here's everything:
>
> 1. **Your unlock code:** [PROMO CODE] — redeem: App Store → your profile icon → "Redeem Gift Card or Code".
>    Download Chord Colors first (it's free), then redeem to unlock everything.
> 2. **One-page brief attached** (the only hard rules: colors on screen with sound in the first
>    3 seconds, mention it's free, use the app's own audio).
> 3. **Timeline:** posted within 10 days. Send me a preview before posting if you want notes —
>    I'll only ever have opinions about the first 2 seconds.
> 4. **Payment:** $[X] via PayPal or Venmo the day it's live. Send your handle.
> 5. **One extra step that's part of the deal:** after posting, send me a Spark Ads authorization
>    code (60 days) so I can boost your post with my ad budget — you keep all views/followers.
>    How: open your video → "..." menu → Ad settings → toggle "Ads authorization" → Generate code
>    → set 60 days → send me the code.
>
> Questions welcome. Excited to see your take!

## 2. They counter higher

Accept up to +$50 over your offer if their engagement ratio was 50x+ (check creator-shortlist.csv).
Otherwise:

> I'm an indie dev funding this out of pocket, so $[X+25] is my ceiling right now. If your video
> performs and I boost it, I'd love to do a multi-video deal at a higher rate next round.

## 3. They ask "what should I make?"

> Totally your call — that's the point of hiring you. If you want a starting point: film yourself
> playing a 4-chord progression, then show the app coloring those same chords, and react to the
> moment it clicks. The one thing I know works: colors + sound in the first 3 seconds.

## 4. Preview needs notes (only ever the hook)

> This is great. One note: the first 2 seconds — [get the colors on screen sooner / start with
> the question, not the intro]. First 2 seconds decide everything on TikTok. Everything else: ship it.

## 5. After they post

> It's live and it's great — payment sent via [PayPal/Venmo]. 🎉
> Send the Spark code when you have a sec (video → "..." → Ad settings → Ads authorization →
> Generate 60-day code) and I'll start putting budget behind it this week.

## 6. Ghosted after yes (7+ days)

> Hey — no stress if life got busy. If you're still in, just confirm and take another week.
> Otherwise I'll free up the promo code, and the door's open next round.

## Notes for you
- Track every state change in `creator-shortlist.csv` (status column: emailed / replied / yes /
  code-sent / posted / paid / spark-live).
- Keep any one creator under $600/year total, or you'll need to collect a W-9 and file a 1099
  (US creators). Under $600 = no paperwork.
- Pay only on posted-video + spark-code, never upfront.
