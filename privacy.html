Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Chord Colors, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use for our free app, Chord Colors.

Data Collection

While we do not collect personal data directly from our users, we do use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting. These services may collect certain non-personal information to help us improve the app's performance and user experience.

Analytics

We use Amplitude for event tracking to collect anonymous usage data. This information helps us understand how users interact with our app and make improvements. The data collected may include:

  • App usage statistics
  • Device information (e.g., device model, operating system version)
  • General location data (country or region)
  • Feature usage and interactions within the app

This data is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify individual users.

Crash Reporting

We use Sentry to collect crash reports and diagnose issues with the app. This service may collect information about your device and the state of the app at the time of a crash. This data is used solely for improving app stability and performance.

In-App Purchases

Chord Colors offers an optional in-app purchase to unlock all features. This transaction is processed through the app store platform (e.g., Apple App Store) and we do not receive or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Amplitude (for event tracking)
  • Sentry (for crash reporting)

These services collect anonymous data as described above. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information on their data handling practices.

Data Retention and Deletion

The anonymous data collected by our analytics and crash reporting services is retained for a period necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. As we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to delete upon request.

Your Choices

While we do not offer a direct opt-out mechanism for analytics within the app, you can limit data collection by adjusting your device settings or using features provided by your mobile operating system to limit ad tracking or reset your advertising identifier.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the app if necessary.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at chordcolors@gmail.com.
