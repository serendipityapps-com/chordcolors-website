# Chord Colors — Marketing Kit (self-contained, no AI needed)

Built 2026-07-04. Everything here is ready to execute solo. Files:

| File | What it is |
|---|---|
| `creator-shortlist.csv` | 32 vetted TikTok piano/guitar creators (from a 216-profile scan), tiered, with stats, contacts, offers. **Use the `status` column as your CRM.** |
| `outreach-emails.md` | 13 personalized ready-to-paste emails (tier 1: creators with public emails) |
| `outreach-dms.md` | 12 copy-paste DMs for the explosive nano creators (tier 2) |
| `creator-brief.md` | The brief you send after a YES (also works for Billo / Creative Challenge) |
| `creator-reply-templates.md` | Every reply you'll need: yes/counter/notes/payment/Spark code/ghosting |
| `tiktok-ads-runbook.md` | Credit registration (ORDER MATTERS), Spark Ads setup, kill/scale rules, Billo fallback |
| `fiverr-app-preview-brief.md` | App Preview editor brief + storyboard + what to screen-record |
| `app-store-featuring-pitch.md` | Paste-ready featuring nomination answers |
| `aso-notes.md` | Listing changes: subtitle, keywords, screenshots, ratings goal |
| `content-calendar.md` | 10 no-face video concepts + 2-week posting calendar |

## The play, in order

**Day 0 (app update approved):**
1. App Store Connect: generate **10 IAP promo codes** (IAP → Promo Codes; they expire in 28 days
   — small batches). Keep the .txt file next to the shortlist CSV.
2. Submit the featuring nomination (`app-store-featuring-pitch.md`). 15 min.
3. Send all 13 emails (`outreach-emails.md`). Mark `status=emailed` in the CSV.
4. Send the 12 DMs from your phone (`outreach-dms.md`). Mark `status=dmed`.
5. Record the 5 screen captures (`fiverr-app-preview-brief.md` bottom section) and order the
   Fiverr edit.

**Days 1–7 (replies come in):**
6. Handle every reply with `creator-reply-templates.md`. YES → send promo code + brief same day.
7. First YES → **that day**, register for the TikTok incentive THEN create the ads account
   (`tiktok-ads-runbook.md` Phase A — the order and the 30-day clock matter).
8. Start posting organic on your own @chordcolors account (`content-calendar.md`) — 3–4/week.
   This also makes your account look alive when creators' audiences check the tag.

**Days 7–21 (videos land):**
9. Each posted creator video: pay same day, collect the 60-day Spark code, boost at $20–25/day
   (`tiktok-ads-runbook.md` Phase B). Kill at $50 if dead; scale winners +50% every 2 days.
10. Upload the finished App Preview video to the listing; apply `aso-notes.md` changes with your
    next app submission.

**Judging it (day ~30):**
- App Store Connect → App Units trend vs. pre-campaign baseline.
- Per-creator: which video drove the visible install bumps → book those creators for round 2
  (multi-video deals at slightly higher rates).
- If <3 creators said yes total: widen to the next 20 in the CSV (sort by likes_per_follower),
  bump offers +$25, and open a Billo order as the parallel track.

## Budget guardrails
- Creator fees round 1: ~$700–1,200 (only paid on posted video)
- TikTok media: $500 out of pocket → +$500 credit match
- Fiverr edit: ~$75 · Apple Ads: $0 (the $100 credit)
- **Hard stop: ~$2k out of pocket for round 1. Nothing here has upfront commitments beyond that.**

## What was deliberately NOT done yet (needs momentum first)
- Google/Reddit ad accounts (don't start their credit clocks until TikTok proves out)
- Web playground share-cards/OG images (good growth engineering for later — every shared
  progression link becomes a colorful preview card; ask an AI session to build it in the
  chordcolors.com web app repo)
- Product Hunt / Show HN launch (do it a week AFTER the ratings improve — you only launch once)
