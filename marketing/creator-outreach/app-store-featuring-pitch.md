**App:** Chord Colors: Piano and Guitar (id 1422598441)

**What makes your app unique / noteworthy? (core pitch)**

> Chord Colors makes music theory visible. It maps every chord and scale to colors derived from
> the circle of fifths — similar colors sound consonant, clashing colors sound tense — so
> beginners can *see* why a progression works before they can name it. No notation, no jargon
> wall: 113 chords, 1,882 guitar voicings, 470 progressions organized by genre, and 148 scales
> from musical traditions around the world, all explorable by eye and ear. It's built natively
> for iOS by a solo independent developer, is free to download with a single one-time unlock
> (no subscription, no ads), and includes a browser demo so anyone can try the concept in
> seconds. The color system doubles as an accessibility path into theory for learners who
> bounce off traditional notation — including self-taught and neurodivergent musicians who
> think visually.
