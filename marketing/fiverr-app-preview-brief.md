# Fiverr brief — App Preview video (paste into a Fiverr "app preview video" gig, ~$50–100)

**Job post / message to seller:**

> I need a 25-second App Store "App Preview" video for my iOS music app, Chord Colors
> (apps.apple.com/us/app/chord-colors-piano-and-guitar/id1422598441).
>
> I'll provide raw screen recordings captured on-device. I need you to: cut to the storyboard
> below, add short text captions, keep the app's own audio as the soundtrack (do NOT add stock
> music — the app's sound is the product), and deliver to Apple's App Preview spec
> (9:16, 1080×1920 or device-native, 15–30s, .mov or .mp4, no hands/fingers overlays, no
> device frames, first frame must look good as a static poster frame).
>
> Storyboard (25s):
> - 0–3s: Progression playing, colors pulsing with each chord. Caption: "See why chords work."
> - 3–8s: Circle of fifths → colors mapping. Caption: "Every key is a color."
> - 8–14s: Browsing 470 progressions by genre, tap one, it plays. Caption: "470 progressions to steal."
> - 14–19s: Guitar voicings screen, tap through voicings. Caption: "1,882 guitar voicings."
> - 19–25s: Scales from around the world playing. Caption: "Free on the App Store."
>
> Deliverables: final video + editable project file. One revision round included.

**Raw footage you need to record first (on iPhone, Settings → Control Center → Screen Recording):**
1. 30s: play a I–V–vi–IV progression, let colors react (this is the money shot — do a few takes)
2. 20s: open circle of fifths / color mapping view
3. 30s: scroll progressions list, tap into 2–3, let each play
4. 20s: guitar voicings screen, tap through voicings
5. 20s: pick an exotic scale, play it
Record with app sound ON, notifications OFF (Do Not Disturb), full battery icon hidden if possible
(record in a clean state — Apple rejects previews showing notification banners).

**After delivery:** App Store Connect → your app → the version's media section → drag the video
into the App Preview slot ahead of screenshot 1. It autoplays muted in search results — the
colors still sell it silently.
